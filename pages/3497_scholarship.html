German-American Society of Pinellas County

Annual College Scholarship

Each year we award a $2,000 scholarship to a deserving student pursuing higher education — because investing in our community's next generation is part of who we are.

🎓 $2,000 Award 📅 Deadline: May 31 👥 Members & Their Families

About the Scholarship

Supporting students who are part of our community

💰

$2,000 Award

Paid over four years at $500 per year, provided all scholarship requirements are maintained.

👥

Eligibility

Open to current members of the Society and their immediate family members. No German heritage requirement — just a connection to our community.

📅

Deadline

Applications are due by May 31st each year. Late applications will not be considered.

📬

How to Submit

Email your completed application package to ilsekearney@yahoo.com by May 31st.

How to Apply

Four documents — download, complete, and email them all together

1

Application Form

The main application — personal details, education history, academic achievements, extracurricular activities, and your participation in German cultural events, programs, or classes.

⬇ Download Application Form
2

Scholarship Essay

Tell us why you feel you should receive this scholarship. Include your career objectives and goals.

⬇ Download Essay Form
3

Letter of Recommendation

A completed recommendation letter from a Society member who is supporting your application.

⬇ Download Recommendation Form
4

Statement of Accuracy

A signed statement confirming that all information in your application is accurate and complete, and that you understand the scholarship requirements.

⬇ Download Statement of Accuracy

Once all four documents are complete, email them as a package to:

ilsekearney@yahoo.com

All applications must be received by May 31st.
