Come Craft With Us

Every other Sunday afternoon, a group of German-American Society members gathers to work on crafts — together. There's no agenda, no project you're required to bring, and no skill level requirement. Pull up a chair, unpack whatever you're working on, and enjoy a few hours of creative community in good company.

We sew, knit, crochet, embroider, repair costumes, scan and archive historical photos — if it's creative, it counts. Welton Brewing Co. & Oyster Bar is right on-site when you need a break. The only ask: bring a snack to share.

No Cost. Just Craft.

Members Only · Every Other Sunday · 1:00 – 4:00 PM

No entry fee. No materials fee. No minimum skill level. Just show up with a project (or without one) and enjoy the company. Membership in the German-American Society is required — crafting is a member perk.

What We Do

A little bit of everything — as long as it's creative

🪡

Costumes

We make and repair costumes for the Krampus Verein and the Gulf Coast Enzianer Schuhplattler Verein — hand-stitched details, alterations, full repairs on traditional garb. There's always something that needs a needle and thread.

🛍️

Fundraising Crafts

We craft handmade items the Schuhplattlers sell at events to support the group. A great way to put your skills to work for the community while spending an afternoon making something.

📐

Skills & Learning

Want to learn to hem? Try a new stitch? Tackle something you've never attempted before? This is the place. We learn from each other — experienced crafters teach, beginners absorb, everyone improves.

✨

Your Own Project

Knitting, crocheting, embroidery, cross-stitch, scanning family photos for the archive, or anything else creative — bring whatever you're working on. If it counts as crafting to you, it counts here.

Want to Join Us?

The easiest way to stay updated on upcoming dates and what we're working on is to join our WhatsApp group. You can also reach out directly to our organizer, Susanne McColley, at enzianmadl@gmail.com.

Not a member yet? Join the German-American Society — crafting is just one of the many perks of membership.
