Euchre Night

Every third Thursday of the month, we gather at the German-American Society for a night of Euchre — one of the great American card games, with deep roots in German immigrant communities. Whether you're a seasoned pro who can call trump in your sleep or you've never held a bower in your life, you're welcome at the table.

Our bar is open, and Welton Brewing Co. & Oyster Bar is right on-site for food. Grab a stein, find a seat, and get ready for a great time.

🃏

We Play for Fun. Full Stop.

No Cash Prizes · No Buy-Ins · No Entry Fees

There is no money on the table at Euchre Night. No cash prize at the end of the night, no buy-in, no entry fee. We play for the love of the game, the camaraderie, and the community. If you're looking for a genuinely fun night out with good people, pull up a chair.

No membership required. Just bring yourself — and a friend if you've got one.

How We Play

Standard Euchre with one house rule you need to know

🃏

Standard Euchre

We play classic four-player partnership Euchre — 24-card deck, trump, the right and left bower. If you know the game, you know the game.

📌

Stick the Dealer

We play with the Stick the Dealer rule — if all four players pass on calling trump, the dealer is forced to call. No one gets off the hook.

🆕

Never Played?

No problem. Euchre is easy to learn and we're happy to teach you. Show up, introduce yourself, and we'll get you playing in minutes.

📅
