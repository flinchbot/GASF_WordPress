Where Fun Meets Gemütlichkeit

Oktoberfest at the German-American Society is a high-energy celebration of German and Bavarian culture, featuring live music, folk dancing, stein-holding contests, and a Kinder Corner packed with kids’ activities. The event offers fun for all ages in a lively, welcoming atmosphere.

Guests enjoy scratch-made German favorites like crispy Schnitzel, hearty sides, and rich desserts. Traditional Oktoberfest brews flow freely, pairing perfectly with the food and festivities. Whether under the outdoor tent or in the Main Hall, it’s a vibrant, flavor-filled celebration of community.
