Welcome to Our Gasthaus

Step into the heart of German hospitality at the German-American Society’s restaurant, where our food is a celebration of tradition, flavor, and Gemütlichkeit.

From hand-breaded schnitzel to rotating regional specials, our kitchen serves up authentic German fare with warmth and flair. Whether you're a longtime member or a first-time guest, you’ll be greeted like family—with smiles, schnitzel, and warm service from our volunteer staff. And don't forget to top off your dinner with our made-from-scratch German desserts!

What to Expect

  • Dinner Service: Second and fourth Saturdays from 6–8 PM — Enjoy classic dishes like bratwurst, sauerkraut, and our famous schnitzel, all prepared fresh in-house. And finish it off with authentic and scrumptious dessert.
  • Family-Friendly Atmosphere: No membership required. Just bring your appetite and good cheer. [associate_fee]
  • Group Reservations: Hosting a club, group, or family gathering? Reserve a table and let us welcome you in style.
