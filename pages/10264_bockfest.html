Bock beer wasn't born in a taproom.
It was brewed by Bavarian monks in the dead of winter.

Rich, dark, and strong enough to sustain them through fasting — bock beer has been a symbol of endurance, craft, and celebration for centuries. Every spring, bock season arrives and steins are raised across Germany and beyond. At our Bock Fest, we honor that tradition with local craft breweries and legendary German imports side by side.

🔥

Bierstacheln

The Hot Poker Ritual — Get Poked

Our signature experience: a red-hot iron poker, pulled glowing from the fire and plunged directly into your bock. The heat caramelizes the malt sugars, creates a smoky foam, and transforms your beer into something entirely different — warmer, richer, and unmistakably dramatic. It's been done in Bavarian taverns for centuries. Now it's your turn.
