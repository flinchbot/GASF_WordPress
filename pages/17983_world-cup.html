🅿️ Getting Here & Parking

8098 66th Street North, Pinellas Park, FL 33781

Our watch parties draw a big crowd and on-site parking fills up fast. We recommend arriving early or using Uber, Lyft, or a taxi.

🚌 Take the bus: PSTA Route 66 runs right along 66th Street North with a stop directly in front of the club. It connects Largo Transit Center and downtown St. Petersburg (Grand Central Station), about every 30 minutes on weekdays and Saturdays (hourly on Sundays). View the Route 66 schedule & map »

Do not park at the Zimring office park (north of the venue) or at 8200 66th Street North (Southern Technical Institute / Café on the Bayou). You will be towed.

Overflow parking at the medical complex (corner of 66th Street North & 78th Ave North) is available for games that kick off after 5:30 PM on weekdays, and anytime on weekends. For weekday games starting before 5:30 PM, those neighboring lots are in use by local businesses during the day — please carpool, use a rideshare, or take a taxi instead so we don't disrupt them.

A Soccer Home, Not a Generic Sports Bar

The German-American Society has been a soccer-focused club for a long time, hosting 50+ soccer watch parties every year. We're the home of the official FC Bayern Munich fan club and show all of their matches, along with select Bundesliga, Champions League, and Men's and Women's World Cup games. Because we aren't a generic sports bar, we're focused on delivering the absolute best watch experience for soccer fans — the right matches, the right atmosphere, and a crowd that lives for the game.
