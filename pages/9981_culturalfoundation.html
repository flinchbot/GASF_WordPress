Our Mission

Our mission is to support the German-American Society Friendship of Pinellas County in sharing German culture through education, authentic dancing, festivals, and Gemütlichkeit — by developing impactful programs, securing funding sources, and building strong relationships with our stakeholders.

Why Your Donation Matters

📚

Education

Supporting German language, history, and cultural education programs for all ages in the Tampa Bay community.

🎓

Scholarship

Funding our annual $2,000 scholarship for members pursuing higher education in fields that support our cultural mission.

🎭

Cultural Programs

Preserving authentic German traditions — Schuhplattler dancing, festivals, music, and the arts — for future generations.

🤝

Community

Building a stronger German-American community in Tampa Bay through outreach, partnerships, and shared heritage.

🍺

Oktoberfest Sponsorship

Interested in sponsoring Tampa Bay's premier German festival? Our Oktoberfest sponsorship packages offer meaningful visibility and community impact for local businesses and organizations.
