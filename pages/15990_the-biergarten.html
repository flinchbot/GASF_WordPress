A Quiet Corner of Bavaria in Pinellas Park

Pull up a chair. Your beer is waiting.

The Biergarten at the German-American Society is an outdoor oasis — a friendly, relaxed space where you can slow down, raise a stein, and enjoy the Florida evening the way it's meant to be enjoyed. Whether you're stopping by after work or making a night of it, this is a place to unwind.

We feature German beers on tap as well as a rotating selection of bottled options — from crisp Pilsners to malty Dunkels to seasonal specialties. We also carry wine and schnapps for those who prefer something different. If you love discovering unique German beers in a relaxed environment, you've found your spot.
