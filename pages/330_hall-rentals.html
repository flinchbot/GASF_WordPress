Three Unique Spaces

🥂

The Main Hall

Our largest space — ideal for weddings, receptions, concerts, comedy shows, and galas. Features a full stage with ample electrical hook-ups, a full bar, fully equipped kitchen, and room for large gatherings.

🍺

The Bierstube

A warm, cozy front bar and dining area — perfect for quinceañeras, birthday parties, intimate celebrations, and private dinners. A charming space with its own distinct character.

🌿

The Biergarten

Our spacious outdoor Biergarten is unlike anything else in Pinellas Park — host corporate gatherings, cabaret shows, sports watch parties, or movie nights on our giant 14' screen under the Florida sky.
